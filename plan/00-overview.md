# AI Safety Study Plan — Overview

A structured self-study program for pivoting into AI safety research, targeting a strong application to MATS (and similar programs: Anthropic Fellows, LASR, Pivotal, SPAR, MARS) in the next cycle.

## The shape of the plan

**~5 hours/day, 5 days/week**, in three tracks:

| Track | Time | Content |
|---|---|---|
| [ML Foundations](plan.html?doc=01-ml-foundations) | 2 hrs/day (daytime) | Math Academy — Mathematical Foundations I–III → Mathematics for ML |
| [Mechanistic Interpretability](plan.html?doc=02-mech-interp) | 2 hrs/day (daytime) | ARENA → mini-projects → public research write-ups |
| [Philosophy](plan.html?doc=03-philosophy) | 1 hr/day (evening) | Primary texts from classical foundations to AI-safety ethics, plus a human-flourishing/autonomy module (Cosmos Institute) |

## Principles

1. **Minimum viable foundations, then research.** The foundations phases have hard caps. The credential that matters for MATS is a crisp, skeptical, time-boxed research write-up — so the plan optimizes for producing those early and often.
2. **Equal parts philosophy and empirical work.** The daytime block builds empirical research skill; the evening block builds the conceptual foundation to reason clearly about why and toward what end.
3. **Practical, not completionist.** Primary texts where they're load-bearing, excerpts and secondary sources everywhere else.
4. **Public accountability.** Every session is logged here — daily notes, lessons learned, and a timelapse of each work session, posted daily to X.

## Rhythm

- **Mon–Fri daytime:** 2 hrs Math Academy + 2 hrs mech interp (Fridays: consolidation, post-mortems, research log).
- **Every evening:** 1 hr philosophy reading + note-taking.
- **Daily wrap-up:** `scripts/end-day.sh` builds the timelapse, updates the log, pushes, and posts to X.

## Timeline at a glance (9 months)

- **Months 1–2:** ML math foundations + mech interp Phase 1 (ARENA); philosophy: classical foundations.
- **Months 3–4.5:** Mech interp mini-projects; philosophy: modern ethics + metaethics.
- **Months 5–7:** One full research project with a public write-up; philosophy: contemporary AI-safety-relevant work.
- **Months 7–9:** MATS application dry-runs (time-boxed 12-hr research tasks + write-ups); apply broadly.
